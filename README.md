# Hopfield Network
Unpublished work by Joshua Oon Soo Goh in fulfillment of Psych 591 Neural Network Modeling course conducted in Spring 2007 at the Dept. of Psychology, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, IL, USA, instructed by John Hummel. This work extends the Hopfield Network as implemented by John Hopfield [1] by evaluating its performance with respect to stimuli repetition.

**Bibliography**<br>
1. Hopfield, J. J. (1982). Neural networks and physical systems with emergent collective computational abilities. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences USA, 79, 2554-2558.
